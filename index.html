About me As an experienced mathematician whose research focus is currently in the field of data science, I have a strong analytical mindset and in-depth knowledge of mathematical modeling and statistics. I am passionate about developing and deploying machine learning and data analytics models. With my knowledge of Python, R, SQL and PowerBI as well as modern technologies such as Hadoop and TensorFlow, I strive to solve complex problems through data-driven approaches and gain valuable insights to optimize business processes. I look forward to contributing and continuously developing my expertise and technical know-how in a research and innovation-oriented team.
Education I received my Bachelor degree in Mathematics in 2004 and my Master degree in Mathematics in 2006 from the VNU-Hanoi University of Sciences. Both these degrees are under the advisory of Prof. Dr. Nguyen Van Minh. In 2012, I received my Ph.D. degree in applied mathematics from Leipzig Univeristy under the advisory of Prof. Dr. Juergen Jost.
